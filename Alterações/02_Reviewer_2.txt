The manuscript appears to address a research topic in brain–computer interfaces, neurophysiology, and 
multi-modal biosignal analysis, as evidenced by extensive references to EEG, EMG, fNIRS, cognitive 
workload datasets, and deep learning-based signal processing. A comprehensive catalog and technical 
mapping of 96 brain-monitoring devices (125 if variants included), using PRISMA methodology and OSF 
registration. This topic is highly relevant and aligns well with the scope of the IEEE Access Journal. However, 
some concerns regarding your manuscript need to be addressed as follows: 
 
1. Manter o escopo geral do artigo (catálogo + mapeamento técnico). 
2. Tornar explícito que o foco é mapeamento técnico e adoção, não validação clínica direta. 
3. Reforçar no texto que o artigo é um scoping review orientado à tomada de decisão, não um 
benchmark experimental. 
→ Onde: Introduction, Discussion
