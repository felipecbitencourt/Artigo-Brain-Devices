TEXTO INTEGRADO PARA INTRODUCTION (R2C1):
================================================================================

"Despite their growing potential, selecting the most appropriate brain 
monitoring device remains a major challenge. The commercial landscape has 
expanded rapidly, encompassing numerous manufacturers with heterogeneous 
specifications. This diversity, coupled with the absence of standardized 
reporting, creates a paradox of choice that complicates decision-making.

This scoping review addresses the following research question: How can 
commercially available portable EEG and fNIRS devices be systematically 
characterized and compared to support informed selection for neuroscience 
and neurorehabilitation research?

Rather than testing a hypothesis, this study provides an evidence-based 
framework that bridges engineering specifications and practical research 
implementation."
