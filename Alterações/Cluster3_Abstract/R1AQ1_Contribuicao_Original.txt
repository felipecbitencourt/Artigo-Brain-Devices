TEXTO PARA MANUSCRITO - CONCLUSION
================================================================================

This review provides the most comprehensive device-level mapping of portable brain monitoring technologies 
currently available in the peer-reviewed literature. The compiled catalog, encompassing 96 devices from 64 
manufacturers across 26 countries, offers an evidence-based framework to guide device selection, bridging the 
gap between engineering specifications and practical research implementation.
